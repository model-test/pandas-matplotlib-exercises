# Student Exam Score Data

This exercise demonstrates using Pandas + Matplotlib to visualize student exam score information

1. Average Exam Score by Major
   - Bar chart visualizing avearge exam scores between students majoring in Computer Science, Math, and Physics.
   - Students majoring in Physics have a higher average exam score than students majoring in Computer Science and Math.
2. Study Hours vs Exam Score
   - Scatter plot visualizing correlation between study hours and exam scores
   - Positive correlation suggesting higher study hours result in higher exam scores
